Polar Bear Wrestling

Polar Bear Wrestling is an extreme sport where participants engage in hand-to-hand combat with polar bears in their natural habitat. This high-risk activity demands advanced wrestling skills, careful planning, and constant monitoring for safety.

Participants experience the thrill of wrestling with one of the world's most powerful predators. Polar Bear Wrestling is an adrenaline-pumping niche within the wrestling realm, offering a unique and electrifying challenge against nature's forces.
